School Assemblies & Workshops

Engaging Science Education

Our school programs are designed to meet state and national science standards while providing an unforgettable experience for students of all ages. We bring a diverse selection of live reptiles and amphibians directly to your classroom or assembly hall.

Key Features

  • STEM-aligned curriculum
  • 10-12 live animal ambassadors
  • Interactive handling opportunities
  • Educational handouts provided

Session Timing

Standard assemblies last 60 minutes. We also offer 45-minute sessions for younger grades or 90-minute intensive workshops for advanced science classes.

Event Preparation Checklist

To ensure the best possible experience for your guests and the safety of our ambassadors, please review the following requirements:

Indoor Space

A climate-controlled area (68-80°F) is required for all sessions involving reptiles.

Presentation Area

A sturdy 6-foot table and at least 10 feet of space between the table and the front row.

Power Access

One standard electrical outlet within 15 feet of the presentation area.
