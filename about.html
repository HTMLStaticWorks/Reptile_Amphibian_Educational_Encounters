Our Story

Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Humans and Herpetology

At Scales & Skin, we believe that education is the most powerful tool for conservation. Our goal is to inspire curiosity and respect for reptiles and amphibians by providing safe, hands-on learning experiences.

Since 2011, we have reached over 100,000 students across the region, dispelling myths and fostering a deeper understanding of these vital components of our ecosystem.
