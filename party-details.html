Unforgettable Kids' Parties 🎈

The ultimate wildlife birthday adventure!

A Birthday They'll Never Forget

Make your child's special day extraordinary with a personalized wildlife encounter! Our birthday party packages bring the wonders of nature right to your home, local park, or chosen venue, providing a perfect blend of entertainment and education.

What's Included in the Experience?

Our party packages are packed with exciting activities designed to engage kids and adults alike:

  • 10+ Animal Ambassadors: A diverse mix of snakes, lizards, tortoises, and amphibians.
  • Engaging Educational Talks: Fun, age-appropriate facts and stories about each animal's diet, habitat, and unique quirks.
  • Handling Experiences: Every guest gets the opportunity to safely touch or hold our friendly ambassadors.
  • Photo Opportunities: Plenty of time for parents to snap the perfect photo of the birthday child holding their favorite reptile.

Safety First

We adhere to the highest safety standards. All animal handling is strictly supervised by our professional educators. We also provide mandatory hand sanitization for all guests before and after touching any animal to ensure everyone's health and safety.

Session Timing

Our birthday party packages typically run between 60 to 90 minutes, providing ample time for the presentation, individual handling, and photos without feeling rushed.

Party Preparation Checklist

To ensure the best possible experience for the kids and our animals, please prepare the following:

Clear Area

We need an open space (approx. 8x8 feet) for the presenter and animals to move safely.

Temperature Control

If indoors, a comfortable room temperature (68-80°F). If outdoors, full shade is strictly required.

Keep Pets Away

Please secure all family dogs and cats in a separate room during the presentation.
